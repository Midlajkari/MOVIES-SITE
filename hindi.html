RAONE (2011)

Ra.One" is a 2011 Indian science fiction superhero film directed by Anubhav Sinha and produced by Gauri Khan. It stars Shah Rukh Khan, Kareena Kapoor, and Arjun Rampal in lead roles, with Armaan Verma, Shahana Goswami, and Tom Wu in supporting roles. The film is notable for its use of special effects, a substantial budget, and its attempt to blend traditional Bollywood elements with a futuristic superhero narrative.

Kabhi Khushi Kabhie Gham...

Kabhi Khushi Kabhie Gham..." is a popular Bollywood film released in 2001, directed by Karan Johar. The title translates to "Sometimes Happiness, Sometimes Sorrow" in English. It's a family drama that revolves around themes of love, family bonds, and societal expectations. The film boasts an ensemble cast including Amitabh Bachchan, Jaya Bachchan, Shah Rukh Khan, Kajol, Hrithik Roshan, and Kareena Kapoor. The storyline follows the wealthy Raichand family, headed by Yashvardhan Raichand (played by Amitabh Bachchan) and his wife Nandini (played by Jaya Bachchan). Their elder son, Rahul (played by Shah Rukh Khan), falls in love with a girl from a lower socioeconomic background, Anjali (played by Kajol), which leads to familial conflict and his expulsion from the household.

Maidaan (2024)

"Maidaan" is a 2024 Indian biographical sports drama directed by Amit Ravindernath Sharma, featuring Ajay Devgn in the lead role. The film tells the inspiring story of Syed Abdul Rahim, who was the coach and manager of the Indian national football team during its golden era in the 1950s and early 1960s. Under his leadership, India achieved significant milestones, including reaching the semi-finals in the 1956 Melbourne Olympics and winning gold at the Asian Games in 1951 and 1962

Blank (2019)

Blank" is a 2019 Indian Hindi-language action thriller film directed by Behzad Khambata. The movie features Sunny Deol, Karan Kapadia (in his debut role), Karanvir Sharma, and Ishita Dutta . The plot centers around a suicide bomber named Hanif, played by Karan Kapadia, who loses his memory in an accident. This incident leaves him with a live bomb attached to his chest, and it's up to Anti -Terrorism Squad Chief SS Dewan, portrayed by Sunny Deol, to defuse the bomb and prevent a potential catastrophe
