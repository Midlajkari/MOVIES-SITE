Kumbalangi Nights

Kumbalangi Nights is a critically acclaimed 2019 Indian Malayalam-language drama film directed by Madhu C. Narayanan in his directorial debut and written by Syam Pushkaran. Set in the picturesque village of Kumbalangi in Kerala, the film intricately weaves a narrative that explores themes of family, masculinity, mental health, and redemption through the lives of four estranged brothers.




varshanghalkku shesham

"Varshangalkku Shesham" is a Malayalam film directed by Vineeth Sreenivasan. The title translates to "After the Years," reflecting the film's exploration of time and the evolution of the Malayalam film industry. The story spans several decades, beginning in the 1970s and continuing to the present day, capturing the changing landscape of cinema and the lives of those who dream of making it big in the industry.

Maheshinte Prathikaaram (2016)

Maheshinte Prathikaaram" is a 2016 Indian Malayalam-language comedy-drama film directed by Dileesh Pothan in his directorial debut. The film stars Fahadh Faasil as Mahesh, a small-town photographer who seeks revenge after being humiliated in a fight . The story, written by Syam Pushkaran, is inspired by a real incident in Pushkaran's village and is set in the picturesque Idukki district of Kerala.

Aavesham (2024)

Ennu Ninte Moideen (2015)

Ennu Ninte Moideen is a 2015 Malayalam-language biographical romantic drama film directed by R.S. Vimal. The movie is based on the real-life love story of B.P. Moideen and Kanchanamala, which took place in the 1960s in Mukkam, Kerala. The film narrates the tale of Moideen (Prithviraj Sukumaran), a Muslim youth and a socio-political activist, and Kanchanamala (Parvathy Thiruvothu), a Hindu woman from an aristocratic family . Despite deep religious and societal barriers, they fall in love . Their families, however, strongly oppose the relationship, forcing them to remain apart physically while continuing their romance through letters and secret meetings. The story unfolds against the backdrop of their struggle to be together, highlighting their unwavering commitment and the sacrifices they make for love.

"AK Ayyappanum Koshiyum (2020)

Ayyappanum Koshiyum is a 2020 Indian Malayalam-language action thriller film written and directed by Sachy. The film stars Prithviraj Sukumaran and Biju Menon in the lead roles, supported by an ensemble cast. The plot revolves around the intense and escalating conflict between Koshy Kurian (Prithviraj Sukumaran), a wealthy and influential ex-Havildar, and Ayyappan Nair (Biju Menon), a disciplined and upright police officer.

Malik ()

Malik is a 2021 Indian Malayalam-language political crime drama film directed, written, and edited by Mahesh Narayanan. The film stars Fahadh Faasil in the titular role, alongside a supporting cast that includes Nimisha Sajayan, Vinay Forrt, Joju George, Dileesh Pothan, and others. The narrative spans several decades and focuses on themes of politics, power, and social justice

cu soon (2020)

C U Soon is a 2020 Indian Malayalam-language computer screen film written and directed by Mahesh Narayanan. The film, which was released on Amazon Prime Video, features Fahadh Faasil, Roshan Mathew , and Darshana Rajendran in the lead roles. Notably, it is among the first Indian films to be shot and presented in a screenlife format, where the entire story unfolds through computer and smartphone screens.
